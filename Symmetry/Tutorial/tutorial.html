Understanding Symmetry

The goal of the study is to find out how humans perceive symmetries in real world scenes.

After you finish all sections of this tutorial, you will be redirected to the quick background survey.

Once you finish the survey, you will be qualified to start the symmetry labeling HITs with rewards.
